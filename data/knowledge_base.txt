AIR CONDITIONER

Common Problems:
- Dirty air filter
- Refrigerant leak
- Blocked vents
- Compressor issue

Safety:
- Turn off power before inspection
- Do not open electrical panels

PLUMBING

Common Problems:
- Broken pipe
- Loose connection
- Water leakage

Safety:
- Shut off water supply
- Keep electrical devices away

ELECTRICAL

Common Problems:
- Loose wiring
- Circuit overload
- Damaged switch

Safety:
- Turn off main power
- Avoid touching exposed wires
